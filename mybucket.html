MyBucket

OVERVIEW

MyBucket is a mobile app UI designed to give users insightful and personalized control over their precious bucket list. For the onboarding process, MyBucket allows users to select topics they're interested in. Their selections would provide the app with insightful information to give personalized suggestions. Within the app would be an area where users could view and manage their entire bucket list based on categories. To help users fulfill items on their bucket list, the app would notify users of great deals for travel, adventures, and other activities.
