Art

Many have the misconception that to create astonishing art, you have to be born talented. Talent may hold some substance, but the reality is that creating art is a skill which you can greatly improve with practice and effort. Art was definitely a developed interest that grew as I invested more time into learning the technical skills and applying them in a creative outlet.
