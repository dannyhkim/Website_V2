CIBC Internship

OVERVIEW

My co-op experience at CIBC Digital was extremely rewarding and insightful. Being given exposure to an innovative workplace with so much to learn definitely opened up my mind and instilled motivation inside me to work relentlessly and see the end to my contributions. As a UI Designer, I was able to work with technologies such as Adobe Experience Manager, Sketch, Photoshop, and Illustrator to design new webpages on the .COM and maintain webpages by executing changes requested by different lines of businesses. As a CIBC Digital co-op, I was also given the opportunity to work with other co-ops and mentors on a 4 month-long project to develop a solution for greater growth in client mobile banking adoption. In order to recognize and incentivize clients through our mobile app, we developed a prototype of a rewards platform within the native app that would allow users to earn points by completing simple mobile banking transactions. These points could then essentially be redeemed for various rewards options which the client could select from.
