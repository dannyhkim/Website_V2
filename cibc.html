FLEX CO-OP PROJECT

As a CIBC Digital Co-op, I was also given the opportunity to work with other co-ops, a mentor, and a sponsor on a 4 month-long project to develop a solution for greater growth in client mobile banking adoption. In order to recognize and incentivize clients through our mobile app, we developed a prototype of a rewards platform within the CIBC app that would allow users to earn points by completing simple mobile banking transactions. These points could then essentially be redeemed for various rewards options which the client could select from. After building the prototype and developing a business case, we were able to present our idea to a group of executives at CIBC. Overall, this project was a great learning experience where I was able to develop and showcase my design, development, ideation, collaboration, and presentation skills.
